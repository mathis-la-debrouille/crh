# Vayt — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: [DATE]**

## 1. Who we are (data controller)
Vayt ("we", "us") provides an AI email and calendar assistant delivered through WhatsApp. The data controller is **[LEGAL ENTITY — VAYT SAS, SIREN [NUMBER], [ADDRESS], France — or your actual legal status]**. For any privacy request, contact **privacy@vaytai.com**.

## 2. Data we collect
- **Account data:** your name, email address, and WhatsApp phone number.
- **Google user data (with your authorization):**
  - Gmail: the content and metadata of your emails, which we read to generate summaries and draft replies; and email drafts we create on your behalf.
  - Google Calendar: your events, which we read, and events we create at your request.
- **WhatsApp messages:** the messages you exchange with Vayt, used to understand and answer your requests.
- **Payment data:** handled by our payment provider (Stripe). We do **not** store your card details.
- **Technical/usage data:** logs, timestamps, and error information used to operate and secure the service.

## 3. How we use your data
We use your data **only** to provide and operate the service you asked for: reading and summarizing your emails, drafting replies, managing your calendar, sending you briefings and reminders on WhatsApp, processing your subscription, and providing support and security. We do **not** sell your data and we do **not** use it for advertising.

## 4. Google API Services — Limited Use
Vayt's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the **Limited Use** requirements.

In particular:
- We only access your Gmail and Google Calendar data to provide user-facing features of Vayt that you request.
- We do **not** transfer or sell this data to third parties, except as needed to provide the service, for security purposes, or to comply with applicable law.
- We do **not** use Google user data to train, develop, or improve generalized or standalone AI/ML models.
- Humans do **not** read your Google data, except: with your explicit consent (e.g. to support you), for security purposes (e.g. investigating abuse), to comply with applicable law, or where the data has been aggregated and anonymized.

## 5. AI processing
To generate summaries and draft replies, the relevant email and calendar content is processed by our third-party AI provider, **[Anthropic (Claude)]**, acting as a processor on our behalf and under contractual confidentiality and security commitments. This content is used solely to produce your requested output and is not used to train generalized AI models.

## 6. Service providers (sub-processors)
We share data with the following providers strictly to operate the service:

| Provider | Purpose | Data |
|---|---|---|
| Google | Gmail & Calendar access | Google user data |
| [Twilio] | WhatsApp message delivery | Phone number, message content |
| [Stripe] | Payment processing | Billing data (card handled by Stripe) |
| [Anthropic] | AI summaries & drafts | Email/calendar content submitted for processing |
| [Railway / Turso / Vercel] | Hosting & database | All of the above, at rest |

## 7. International transfers
Some providers are located outside the EEA (e.g. in the United States). Where this is the case, transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

## 8. Data retention
We keep your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data within **[e.g. 30 days]**, except where we must retain certain data to comply with legal obligations (e.g. billing records).

## 9. Your rights
Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your data, to restrict or object to processing, and to withdraw consent at any time (including by disconnecting Google or WhatsApp). To exercise these rights, contact **privacy@vaytai.com**. You may also revoke Vayt's access to your Google account at any time via [Google Security settings](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions). You have the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority (in France, the CNIL).

## 10. Security
We use technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit, restricted access, and OAuth tokens stored securely. No system is perfectly secure; report any concern to **security@vaytai.com**.

## 11. Children
Vayt is not intended for anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## 12. Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be notified to you. The "Last updated" date reflects the latest version.

## 13. Contact
Privacy questions or requests: **privacy@vaytai.com**.
